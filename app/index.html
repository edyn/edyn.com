Keep a good thing growing

Edyn keeps you connected to your garden, so you always know precisely what’s needed to keep your plants healthy. So your garden will thrive.

The Edyn Smart Garden System

Stay connected to your garden

The Edyn Garden Sensor continuously monitors conditions in your garden: soil moisture, soil nutrition, humidity, light, and temperature.

Get real-time guidance sent to your phone

The Garden Sensor connects to your Wi-Fi network to send you valuable insights through the Edyn App.

Never overwater or underwater your plants again

The Edyn Water Valve automatically waters your plants based on how much water they really need.
